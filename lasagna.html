Ingrediants

The Ingrediants required for making a Lasagna are :

  • 12 lasagna noodles
    1 large zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped ,3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt, divided, ½ teaspoon black pepper, divided
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese, 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese, ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps :

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and begin to brown. Stir in the dried basil, dried oregano, ½ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon black pepper.
  4. Pour the tomato sauce into the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, remaining salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  5. To assemble, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place 4 lasagna noodles on top. Spread half of the ricotta mixture evenly over the noodles.
  6. Arrange half of the zucchini slices, baby spinach leaves, and mushroom mixture on top. Sprinkle with ⅓ of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers with 4 more lasagna noodles and the remaining ingredients. Top with the last 4 lasagna noodles, the remaining tomato sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes, then garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve slices of this vibrant and delicious lasagna to delight your guests.
